You are a Product Owner

If you manufacture, assemble, or produce goods placed on the Sri Lankan market, you are likely classified as a Product Owner under EPR regulations.

Who is a Product Owner?

A Product Owner (also referred to as a Producer in many EPR frameworks) is any entity that:

  • Manufactures products under its own brand/name
  • Assembles or modifies products before placing them on the market
  • Imports products for distribution or sale in Sri Lanka
  • First places products on the national market (even if manufactured abroad)

As a Product Owner, you are legally responsible for the end-of-life management of your products — including collection, recycling, recovery, and environmentally sound disposal of waste generated from your products.

Your EPR Responsibilities

Under Sri Lanka’s upcoming Extended Producer Responsibility framework, you are required to:

  • Register with the designated Producer Responsibility Organization (PRO)
  • Meet collection and recycling/recovery targets
  • Finance or organize waste management systems
  • Report annually on quantities placed on market and waste managed
  • Provide proper labeling and consumer information
