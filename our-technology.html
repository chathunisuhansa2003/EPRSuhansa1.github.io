Intelligent compliance

We gather data and insights at every point of the journey and, combined with our supply chain expertise, enable retailers to make more informed decisions across their entire operations.

Next-gen stock management

With EPR's advanced supply chain technology, goods can be intelligently moved around the globe. Our platform and portals enable businesses to check, sort, and move stock information in real-time, ensuring they are compliant and correct before sending to retailers.

Supply chain accountability

Our technology provides new levels of transparency for retailers by giving teams access to accurate data about what is happening across their supply chain. We develop bespoke software and solutions that are tailored to each retailer's individual set-up, designed to increase transparency and operational efficiencies – a foundation for improving sustainability.

End-to-end waste management

EPR's end-to-end waste management has digitised the process of managing waste from end-to-end, replacing manual processes with automation. Our customers can see from the moment they place an order, managing fulfilment, order amends, data, documentation, and invoicing all in one place.
