Our services

PIBO

Product Owners

Product owners are manufacturers responsible for the entire lifecycle of their products, including end-of-life management under EPR regulations to promote sustainability.

Check Your Role

Brand Owners

Brand owners manage products sold under their brand name, ensuring compliance with EPR by handling recycling and waste management for environmental responsibility.

Check Your Role

Importers

Importers bring products into the market and must adhere to EPR guidelines, taking accountability for the environmental impact of imported goods through proper disposal and recycling.

Industries We Serve

We provide tailored EPR compliance and circular economy solutions across key sectors

Electrical and Electronic

Supporting producers of appliances, IT equipment, lighting, and consumer electronics with full WEEE/E-waste compliance and responsible recycling.

Electronic Vehicles & Hybrid Batteries

Specialized EPR services for EV and hybrid battery producers, covering collection, safe dismantling, and material recovery.

Solar and Renewable Energy

Helping solar panel, inverter, and renewable energy equipment producers meet end-of-life obligations and maximize material circularity.

Medical

Compliance solutions for medical device and healthcare product manufacturers, ensuring safe disposal of hazardous and non-hazardous waste.

Chemical Industries

Managing EPR for chemical packaging, containers, and hazardous substances with strict environmental and safety standards.

Fabric and Garments

Supporting textile and apparel brands with textile waste collection, recycling, and circular fashion initiatives.

ICT

End-to-end EPR support for computers, servers, mobile devices, networking equipment, and data center hardware producers.
