Extended Producer Responsibility Pvt Ltd blends technology with a skilled workforce to help businesses reduce waste and adopt circular practices.

We support companies in shifting from the traditional linear “take make waste” model to a circular economy where growth is decoupled from resource use.

We make circularity practical, scalable, and full of opportunity.

Closing Circularity Gaps in Sri Lanka

Our vision is to become Sri Lanka’s first and most trusted leader in Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR).

With specialist operations and partners across the country, EPR Ltd helps businesses adopt circular economy practices that reduce waste, recover value, and keep materials in use for longer. We deliver a unified circular model tailored for Sri Lanka, built around three core service areas—our ‘loops’—that enable scalable, responsible, and future ready resource management.

What is the circularity gap?

6.9% of resources are kept in use each year

Over 90% are wasted or lost — highlighting the need for circular systems.
