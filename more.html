Our Services

Through EPR Ltd’s “Sri Lanka Recycle Loop”, we provide a full range of recycling and resource management services. Each offering is tailored to your needs. We make circularity easy, practical, and good for business.

What we do for Sri Lanka

  • Make the circular economy accessible: translate complex ideas into practical actions that your team can apply in daily operations.
  • Tailor solutions for Sri Lankan businesses: focus on e-waste management, take-back programs, and regulator-aligned reporting that fit local needs.
  • Enable real-world impact: show measurable improvements in material recovery, compliance, and cost efficiency through easy-to-use data and workflows.

Why this matters for Sri Lanka

  • Compliance and transparency: regulator-ready reports and auditable data that help you stay ahead of evolving e-waste rules.
  • Local collaboration: connect importers, manufacturers, retailers, recyclers, and regulators within Sri Lanka’s ecosystem.
  • Everyday value: make circularity a reality in your operations—from procurement and packaging to end-of-life management.

Our approach

  • Practical and simple: we break down circularity into concrete steps you can implement today.
  • Tangible outcomes: clear dashboards, actionable insights, and predictable timelines that translate into real business value.
  • Ready for Sri Lanka: e-waste focused with Sri Lankan regulations, local partners, and regional considerations.
