Simplifying circularity for real-world impact

The Sri Lankan economy is currently only 8.6% circular, resulting in a critical gap that exacerbates environmental, economic, and social challenges. We're here to help businesses move away from the traditional take-make-waste model toward a thriving circular economy.

We do this by making the circular economy accessible — something people can understand and engage with. By taking a practical approach and using smart tools, we help our customers achieve real circularity in their day-to-day operations.

#CloseTheGap with EPR

EPR embraces circularity and the circular economy by helping to close what is known as the circularity gap — the disparity between the volume of materials we extract and the proportion being reused annually.

#CloseTheGap is more than a campaign; it's a movement. It reflects our commitment to helping businesses identify where they can act now and take bold steps to be future-fit. By gaining a clearer understanding of their resources, value chains and resource flows, businesses can make smarter sustainability decisions that reduce waste and regenerate resources.
